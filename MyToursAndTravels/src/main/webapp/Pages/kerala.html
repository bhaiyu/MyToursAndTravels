Tour Details:-

Day 01. Arrival At Cochin Airport and Transfer to Hotel Munnar Arrival at Cochin Apt/Rly. Station, Proceed to Munnar- “The Southern Kashmir of Kerala”. Munnar is nature lover’s paradise, &1800 Mts.above Sea level. On arrival, Check-Inn to the Hotel/Resort and relax. Overnight at Munnar.

Day02:- Munnar After B/fast, go for Sightseeing- Mattupetty Dam/ Kundala Dam for boating. On the way, a visit to Echo point & shooting point. Visit Eravikulam National Park. Overnight at Munnar.

Day 03: Munnar - Thekkady After B/fast, Check-Out from the hotel at Munnar, & proceed to Thekkady, the land of Elephants, unending chains of Hills & spices plantations. Check-Inn to your Hotel/ Resort. Afternoon, if possible boating in Periyar Lake. Through the Wildlife Sanctuary, spices plantation. Overnight at Thekkady.

Day 04:-Thekkady - Alleppey After B/fast, Check-Out from Thekkady & proceed to Alleppey A unique backwater destination C/Into . Full day local sightseen. Overnight at hotel.

Day 05:-Alleppey - Kovalam After B/fast, Check-Out from Alleppey . Than proceed to Kovalam. C/Inn to the Hotel/Resort. Kovalam beach is a sheltered natural bay with cool soothing palms and gentle waves. In the evening you can explore the beach, handicrafts, jewellery, &Cloth shops, spread along the water front. Overnight at Kovalam.

Day 06:- Kovalam After B/fast, full day sightseen Padmanbha swami temple ,puthan maliga palace museum , napier museum overnight in hotel

Day 07: Kovalam - Trivandrum After B/fast, Drop at Trivandrum Airport or Railway station for onward journey.
